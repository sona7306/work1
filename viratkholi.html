Virat Kohli is a legendary Indian cricketer known for his aggressive batting style, remarkable consistency, and leadership.
Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, he has scored over 20,000 international runs and is considered one of the greatest modern-day batsmen.
Kohli has captained India across all formats, leading them to major victories, including the 2018 Test series win in Australia and the 2024 T20 World Cup.
He is also known for his fitness, philanthropy through the Virat Kohli Foundation, and his high-profile marriage to actress Anushka Sharma.
